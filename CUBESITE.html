CUBE|OS

CUBE is a Mini Operating System inside shortcuts made with Jellycuts V1.4.1(5) (made by Zachary Lineman) as more features are added almost every week the possibilities will soon be endless.


A Bit More About CUBE|OS

CUBE is currently in Beta. You will be able to get it, but it won't be a full version or a great one. As there will be a lot of bugs, we ask that you report them at quinndevfacthound@gmail.com
